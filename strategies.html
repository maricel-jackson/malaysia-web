Tips and Strategies

Considering Malaysia's diverse consumer market, digital penetration, and ASEAN membership, the following strategies are likely highly effective:
1. E-commerce platforms – Leverage platforms like Shopee and Lazada.
2. Social media marketing – Use Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok ads to promote.
3. Joint ventures – Partner with local retailers or distributors for easier market access.
4. Localized promotions – Incorporate Malaysian culture, festivals, and language in campaigns.
